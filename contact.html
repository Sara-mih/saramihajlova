ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Sara, and I'm currently a student at the Faculty of Information
and Communication Sciences (FINKI) in Skopje, North Macedonia. I am pursuing a
degree in Information Technology (IT), specifically focusing on Programming and
Information Technology (PIT).

During my time at FINKI, I have had the opportunity to work on various projects that
have honed my programming skills and allowed me to collaborate with like-minded
individuals.

Originally from Stip, a vibrant city known for its rich cultural heritage and friendly
atmosphere, I completed my secondary education at a local gymnasium. Stip holds a
special place in my heart as my hometown, known for its vibrant culture, historical
significance, and warm community spirit.

Looking forward, I am eager to deepen my knowledge in IT, collaborate with peers
on exciting projects, and contribute meaningfully to the field. I believe in
continuous learning and embracing challenges as opportunities for personal and
professional growth.

Feel free to connect with me to discuss technology, education, or anything in between.
I look forward to meeting new people and exploring new ideas together!
